Welcome to Number Punchers!


Instructions:


Each level will provide a math challenge, based on finding primes, mutiples, or factors!


Use the arrow keys to move your Puncher (The Rock) around the board.


Correct answers will reward you with 50 points, and incorrect answers will lose a life.


Finding all of the answers on the level will complete the level, and take you to the next (more difficult) level.


Beware of Dinobot, who is trying to eat you for lunch. Running into Dino will lose a life.


Lose all of your lives, and the game is over!
